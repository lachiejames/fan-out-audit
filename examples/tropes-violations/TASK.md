## Task

Check all user-facing text against the AI writing tropes reference document. Flag any instances of AI writing patterns including but not limited to:

- Magic adverbs ("quietly", "deeply", "fundamentally")
- "Delve" and friends ("leverage", "robust", "streamline", "harness", "utilize")
- Ornate nouns ("tapestry", "landscape", "paradigm", "ecosystem")
- "Serves as" dodge
- Negative parallelism ("It's not X -- it's Y")
- Dramatic countdown ("Not X. Not Y. Just Z.")
- Self-posed rhetorical questions ("The X? A Y.")
- Anaphora/tricolon abuse
- Filler transitions ("It's worth noting", "Importantly", "Notably")
- Superficial analyses ("-ing" phrases tacked on)
- False ranges ("from X to Y")
- Short punchy fragments for manufactured emphasis
- Listicle in a trench coat
- False suspense ("Here's the kicker", "Here's the thing")
- Patronizing analogies ("Think of it as...")
- "Imagine a world where..."
- False vulnerability
- "The truth is simple" assertions
- Grandiose stakes inflation
- Pedagogical voice ("Let's break this down")
- Vague attributions
- Invented concept labels
- Em-dash addiction
- Bold-first bullets
- Unicode decoration (arrows, smart quotes)
- Fractal summaries
- Dead metaphors
- Historical analogy stacking
- One-point dilution
- Signposted conclusions ("In conclusion", "To sum up")
- "Despite its challenges..." formula
